Computer Science and Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology, BSc CSIT course in Nepal affiliated to Tribhuvan University is a four-year course that spans over eight semesters. Students program through each semester by completing the qualitying exams.

Throughout the program, students receive comprehensive education in areas such as:

  • Programming
  • Algorithms
  • Database Management
  • Computer Network
  • Web Development
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) and other relevant subjects
